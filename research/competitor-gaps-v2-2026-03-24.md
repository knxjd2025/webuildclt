## CRITICAL PRIORITY GAPS (Immediate Action)

### Gap 1: "commercial construction cost" + pricing content
- **Competitors ranking:** Abstrakt MG, Autodesk, BhumiCalculator, MannLeeCW
- **Search volume est.:** 2,400/mo (national), 150-300/mo (Charlotte local)
- **Keywords:** `commercial construction cost per square foot`, `commercial building cost Charlotte NC`, `how much does commercial construction cost`, `office build-out cost per square foot Charlotte`
- **Why you're missing:** No pricing/cost guide content. Your service pages describe services but don't answer the #1 question prospects search.
- **Content type needed:** Long-form guide
- **Action:** Create `/guides/commercial-construction-costs-charlotte` — cover cost per sq ft by building type (office, retail, medical, warehouse), factors affecting cost, Charlotte-specific data, cost comparison tables.

### Gap 2: "tenant improvement" / "TI allowance" educational content
- **Competitors ranking:** Nance, J. Forrest Development, Summit, Magnolia
- **Search volume est.:** 1,800/mo (national), 100-200/mo (local)
- **Keywords:** `what is a TI allowance`, `tenant improvement allowance Charlotte`, `how to negotiate tenant improvements`, `commercial lease build out guide`
- **Why you're missing:** You have a `/services/tenant-improvements` page but no educational content explaining TI allowances, negotiation, or the process.
- **Content type needed:** Guide + blog post
- **Action:** Create `/guides/tenant-improvement-allowance-guide-charlotte` explaining TI allowances, typical $/sqft, negotiation tips, landlord vs tenant responsibilities.

### Gap 3: "Charlotte construction permits" / permitting process
- **Competitors ranking:** Mecklenburg County (code.mecknc.gov), various blogs
- **Search volume est.:** 800-1,200/mo (local)
- **Keywords:** `Charlotte NC building permit`, `commercial building permit Charlotte`, `how to get a building permit Charlotte NC`, `Mecklenburg County permit process`
- **Why you're missing:** You have a guide referenced but competitor content is more specific. The county site ranks but is hard to navigate, creating an opportunity for a better UX guide.
- **Content type needed:** Comprehensive guide
- **Action:** Expand/create `/guides/charlotte-building-permit-guide` with step-by-step process, costs, timelines, required documents, links to county portal.
