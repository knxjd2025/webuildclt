## Detailed Competitor Intelligence

### KMD Construction (kmdconstruction.net)
- **Strategy**: Per-city service hubs (Charlotte + Charleston). Each city gets sub-pages for retail contractor, commercial builder, regional builder, general contractor.
- **Unique keywords**: grocery store construction, church construction, shopping center construction, manufacturing plant construction
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: No industry-specific pages for grocery, church, shopping center

### Magnolia Construction (magnoliaconstruction.com)
- **Strategy**: Most aggressive location SEO — 20+ cities across NC and VA. Replicates entire service set per city.
- **Unique keywords**: value engineering, cost estimating, site development, new construction (ground-up)
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: Only 3 area pages vs 20+. Missing site development, value engineering, cost estimating pages.

### GAIS Construction (gaisconstruction.com)
- **Strategy**: Deep niche authority in restaurant/brewery construction. Technical content about specialty flooring, grain handling, boiler systems, fermentation tanks.
- **Unique keywords**: brewery construction, brewery buildout, food and beverage construction, turnkey design and build
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: No brewery-specific content despite restaurant page existing. Charlotte's craft brewery scene is a valuable keyword niche.

### J. Forrest Development (jforrestdevelopment.com)
- **Strategy**: Industry-specific buildout pages + process service pages.
- **Unique keywords**: owner's representative, construction consulting, adaptive reuse, historic renovation, salon/spa buildout, commercial build-out (distinct from "upfit")
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: Missing owner's rep, adaptive reuse, and consulting pages. "Commercial build-out" captures different intent than "commercial upfit."

### Ranger Construction (ranger-construction.com)
- **Strategy**: Commercial upfit specialist with maintenance division targeting property managers.
- **Unique keywords**: property management construction services, ongoing facility maintenance
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: No content targeting property managers/landlords as an audience.

### Gibson Custom Construction (gibsoncustomconstruction.com)
- **Strategy**: Hyperlocal suburb targeting + small business focus.
- **Unique keywords**: small business construction, demolition subcontractor, plus 10+ suburb pages (Gastonia, Huntersville, Mooresville, Cornelius, Davidson, Rock Hill)
- **WeBuildCLT gap**: "Small business construction" is smart audience-focused keyword. Suburb coverage is thin.
