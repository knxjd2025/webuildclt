## Gap Analysis by Category

### 1. Service Page Gaps (CRITICAL)

WeBuildCLT has 7 service pages + we-coat, but competitors have **deeper service hierarchies**:

**KMD Construction** has:
- `/commercial-construction/commercial-builder/`
- `/commercial-construction/regional-builder/`
- `/commercial-contractor/retail-contractor/`
- `/commercial-contractor/regional-contractor/`
- `/commercial-contractor/general-contractor/`

**Magnolia Construction** has:
- `/pre-construction/`
- `/construction-management/`
- `/design-build/`
- `/upfits-and-renovations/commercial-upfits/`
- `/new-construction/`
- Location-specific duplicates of each

**Missing from WeBuildCLT:**
- Pre-construction services page
- Construction management page
- Tenant improvement / commercial renovation page
- Industry-specific pages (restaurant, medical, retail, warehouse)

### 2. Location Page Gaps (HIGH)

WeBuildCLT has 3 area pages (South Charlotte, Lake Norman, Fort Mill). Competitors target many more:
- Huntersville, Cornelius, Davidson, Mooresville (Lake Norman suburbs)
- Matthews, Mint Hill, Indian Land (South Charlotte suburbs)
- Rock Hill, Tega Cay, Lake Wylie (SC border towns)
- Ballantyne, SouthPark, Myers Park (Charlotte neighborhoods)

### 3. Blog/Content Gaps (HIGH)

**Nance Construction** publishes blog content on:
- Community development through commercial projects
- Commercial site selection and planning
- ADA compliance and accessibility upgrades
- Design-build approaches

**Gibson Custom Construction** blogs about:
- Quality commercial upfits (process-focused)
- Small business construction guides

**WeBuildCLT has 26 guides** in `/guides/` but they're static and generic. Missing:
- Process-focused content ("What to expect during a commercial upfit")
- Cost-focused content ("Commercial construction costs in Charlotte 2026")
- Compliance content ("ADA compliance for Charlotte commercial properties")
- Comparison content ("Design-build vs traditional construction in Charlotte")

### 4. Niche/Authority Gaps (MEDIUM)

**Unique WeBuildCLT advantages NOT being leveraged:**
- **USGBC membership** → No sustainable/green building content exists
- **Veteran-owned** → No veteran-owned business content or certifications page
- **Design Center** → No virtual tour content beyond one page
- **60+ years combined experience** → No team/expertise showcase

## Key Takeaway

The **#1 priority** is getting the new Next.js site indexed by Google. WeBuildCLT already has more content than most competitors (7 services + 3 areas + 26 guides + blog). The gap is **indexing and visibility**, not content volume. After indexing, the biggest content gaps are:
1. Pre-construction services page
2. Construction management page
3. Industry-specific niche pages (restaurant, retail, medical)
4. USGBC/sustainability content (unique differentiator)
5. Deeper location targeting (Charlotte neighborhoods and suburbs)
